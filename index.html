The phrase can be used to describe various scenarios where people engage in cunning or deceitful behavior to achieve their goals or manipulate others. It can also refer to playful banter or verbal sparring that involves wit and cleverness. In essence, "Katakuti Khela" captures the essence of strategic or psychological gamesmanship in human interactions.
